🏀 6-7 Game — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 9, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Kray Kray ("we", "us", or "our") handles information in the 6-7 Game mobile application ("the App").

1. Information We Collect

We do not directly collect any personal information from users. The App stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Your best score and best time
  • Your total tap count

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

2. Third Party Services — Google AdMob

The App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads, including:

  • Device identifiers
  • IP address
  • General location data
  • App usage data

For more information on how Google handles data, please visit Google's Privacy Policy.

3. Children's Privacy

We take children's privacy seriously. The App is intended for general audiences including users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Advertisements served through AdMob are configured to comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and applicable Canadian privacy laws.

4. Data Retention

All game data (scores, times, tap counts) is stored locally on your device using Apple's UserDefaults. You can delete this data at any time by uninstalling the App.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Know what data is stored about you
  • Request deletion of your data (by uninstalling the App)
  • Opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
